About 3andiBlasti

Our Journey

3andiBlasti is Morocco's breakthrough FTC robotics team, founded in 2023 with a mission to advance STEM education and innovation in our community. As rookies in the FIRST Tech Challenge, we're proud to represent Morocco in the global robotics arena.

Our name "3andiBlasti" reflects our Moroccan heritage and our innovation mindset - it means "I've got my place" in Darija, our local dialect, symbolizing our determination to carve out our place in the world of competitive robotics.

Our Mission

We aim to inspire the next generation of Moroccan innovators by:

  • Promoting STEM education and robotics in underserved communities
  • Building international collaborations to exchange knowledge and experiences
  • Creating technology solutions that address real-world challenges
  • Developing leadership and technical skills among team members

Community Outreach

At 3andiBlasti, we believe in sharing our passion for STEM and robotics with our community. Through various initiatives, we're working to inspire the next generation of Moroccan innovators.

School Visits

We've visited 15+ schools across Morocco, introducing students to robotics through interactive demonstrations and hands-on activities.

STEM Workshops

Our team has conducted workshops for over 200 students, teaching basic programming, 3D design, and robot building skills.

Mentorship Program

We've established a mentorship program connecting experienced engineers with students interested in pursuing STEM careers.

Community Events

Our team regularly participates in local events, showcasing robotics to the broader community and inspiring interest in technology.
